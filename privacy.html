Direct Care Pediatrics ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. By using our Website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

We may collect the following types of information when you use our Website:
a. Personal Information You Provide
  • Name, phone number, email address (e.g., via contact forms or scheduling pages)
  • Child’s name and age (if submitted for services)
  • Any personal health information (PHI) you voluntarily provide
b. Automatically Collected Information:
  • IP address, browser type, access times
  • Device type and browsing behavior
  • Usage data via cookies or analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)

2. Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide patient support and onboarding communication

If you submit personal health information (PHI) through our Website, it may be protected under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We take appropriate safeguards to protect this information.
However, please note that standard contact forms and email may not be HIPAA-compliant. For secure communication, we may direct you to a patient portal or encrypted messaging system.

We may share your information:

Our Website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather analytics data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly.

Our Website may include links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Website is intended for use by parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.
