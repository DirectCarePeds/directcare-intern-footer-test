Direct Access, Real Care

Parents are tired of waiting weeks for appointments, sitting in germ-filled waiting rooms, and leaving with more questions than answers. Healthcare shouldn’t be this hard.

Our model is simple: text a real pediatrician instead of Googling. Get real answers from a doctor who knows your kids. If testing is needed, we can send a medical assistant or test kit to your home — strep, UTI, RSV, flu, and more. Results come back within 24 hours, all coordinated in the same SMS text thread.

No waiting rooms. No unnecessary exposures. Just fast, personal care that actually works for modern families.
