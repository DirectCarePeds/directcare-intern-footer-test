About Direct Care Pediatrics

Direct Care Pediatrics is a pediatric medical practice based in Sandy, Utah, founded by Dr. Dallin Hubbard, MD, PhD. We provide modern pediatric care through a direct care model that prioritizes access, longer visits, and direct communication between families and their pediatrician.

What We Do

Direct Care Pediatrics offers:

  • Pediatric house calls
  • Virtual pediatric visits
  • Text-based pediatric care
  • Newborn and infant care
  • Preventive and sick visits
  • Lab testing and imaging coordination
  • School and daycare medical documentation

Our care model is designed to help families manage common childhood illnesses, developmental concerns, and preventive care without unnecessary urgent care or emergency room visits.

How Our Care Model Works

Direct Care Pediatrics does not accept traditional health insurance. Families pay transparent monthly memberships or visit-based fees, which allows for:

  • Direct access to a pediatrician via text
  • Same or next-day virtual visits
  • In-home pediatric visits when appropriate
  • Clear, upfront pricing

This model allows physicians to spend more time with families and focus on practical, evidence-based pediatric care.

Areas We Serve

Our clinic is located in Sandy, Utah. We provide pediatric house calls in Sandy and surrounding communities, including Draper, South Jordan, Riverton, Midvale, Cottonwood Heights, and nearby areas. Virtual pediatric visits are available to families throughout the state of Utah.
