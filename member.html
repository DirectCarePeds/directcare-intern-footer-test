What Services are included?



Preventive Care

Well-child & wellness checks, annual physical and age-appropriate screenings.


Acute Visits

Same or next day visits for acute and urgent needs.


Minor Procedures

Sutures, wound care, skin checks, EKGs, ear wax removal, etc.


Disease Management

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment plans focused on the individual's medical needs.


Wholesale Lab Discounts

We work with local labs and radiology to get you the best prices so you can focus on your health, not your wallet.


Telemedicine & Home Visits

Video, text, email your doctor with ease. Travel fee* is $30 per home visit.
*Currently waived within in the service area

What services not included?



Cost of Medications

Prescription medications, vaccines and over-the-counter drugs are not included; however, we work with you to find the best prices.


Specialist Consultations

Consultations with specialists outside of primary care are not covered. OB & gynecology services are not covered.


Emergency Room Visits

Emergency room visits and hospitalizations are not included. We advise all members to aquire a high-deductible or catastrophic insurance plan.

Non-Member Services

For non-members, enjoy on-demand primary care at $300 per patient per doctor's visit without a subscription commitment. We charge a $30 travel fee for home visits within our service area. We are currently waiving the travel fee if you sign up for our membership while our clinic is under renovations. Discounts are available to uninsured, healthshares, high-deductible plan holders, and financially needy.
