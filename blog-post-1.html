What Is Concierge Pediatrics?

“Concierge pediatrics” usually refers to a membership-based model where families get more direct access to their doctor, longer visits, and fewer restrictions. At Direct Care Pediatrics, our model is known as Direct Primary Care (DPC), but it functions exactly like concierge care—without the overhead or confusion of insurance billing.
