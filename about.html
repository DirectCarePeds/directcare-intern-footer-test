Meet the Doctor

Dallin Hubbard

Founder & Pediatrician



Dr. Dallin Hubbard, MD, PhD

Dallin is a pediatrician in Sandy, Utah, who graduated from medical school at the University of Utah and completed his residency at Indiana University with Riley Children's Hospital in Indianapolis. His training included neonatology and complex pediatric medical care. He enjoys helping children with a broad spectrum of needs, from common colds to ADHD and mental health concerns. Dallin is passionate about listening to his patients and is dedicated to finding the best solutions for them.

Driven by his passion for helping children, he started this practice to provide modern, innovative, and individualized pediatric care. As evident by his PhD beyond medical training, he is deeply committed to researching the best treatments for his patients. He is open to discussion of holistic, integrated, as well as traditional evidence-based treatments to provide individualized care for your child.

Outside the clinic, he enjoys mountain biking, hiking, and camping with his wife and seven children. He speaks fluent Portuguese, some Spanish, and is learning Cantonese!

At our practice, we are breaking free from traditional healthcare models and do not accept health insurance. Patients benefit from longer visits and direct access to the doctor via text and email. We proudly work with cash payments, memberships, and all health shares. Our goal is to redefine healthcare for busy families and improve our healthcare system one patient at a time.

Why We Started a Direct Care Clinic:

Starting this direct primary care clinic stems from our passions for transforming healthcare into a more personalized and accessible experience. We believe every person deserves unhurried, individualized attention, and by eliminating the constraints of traditional models, we can focus on building lasting relationships with families. This clinic is a response to the desire for simplicity, transparency, and a direct connection between healthcare providers and families, creating an environment where quality care is at the forefront.

Why We Don't Take Traditional Health Insurance:

Opting not to accept traditional health insurance allows us to maintain a patient-centric focus. By sidestepping the bureaucratic hurdles and administrative complexities associated with insurance, we can allocate more time and resources directly to patient care. This decision aligns with the philosophy of putting the needs of patients and their families first, enabling a streamlined healthcare experience that prioritizes quality over paperwork.

Important Note on Direct Care:

It's crucial to understand that direct primary care is not a replacement for health insurance but rather a valuable supplement, particularly beneficial for those with high deductible plans or underinsured situations. This model caters to families seeking enhanced accessibility, convenience, and a direct partnership in their family's healthcare journey.
