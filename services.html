What Services are Included?

At Direct Care Pediatrics, we mainly focus on providing primary care for children, and occasionally offer basic medical care for adults as a convenience and courtesy for parents with children.


Preventive Care

Well-child & wellness checks, annual physical and age-appropriate screenings.


Acute Visits

Same or next day visits for acute and immediate needs.


Minor Procedures

Sutures, wound care, skin checks, EKGs, ear wax removal, absess drain, toe nail removal, etc.


Disease Management

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment plans focused on the individual's medical needs.


Wholesale Labs

We work with local labs and radiology to get you the best prices so you can focus on your health, not your wallet.


Telemedicine & Home Visits

Video, text, email your doctor with ease. Travel fee* is $50 per home visit.
*Currently waived within in the service area

What services not included?



Cost of Medications

Prescription medications and over-the-counter drugs are not included; however, we work with you to find the best prices.


Specialist Consultations

Consultations with specialists outside of primary care are not covered. OB & gynecology services are not covered.


Emergency Room Visits

Emergency room visits and hospitalizations are not included. We advise all members to aquire a high-deductible or catastrophic insurance plan.

All Kids Fees

For all kids, enjoy on-demand primary care at $300 per month. Discounts are available to uninsured, healthshares, high-deductible plan holders, and financially needy.
