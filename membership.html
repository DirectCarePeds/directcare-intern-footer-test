Become A Member

Discover our transparent membership plans for pediatric and adult primary care. A $100 registration fee per family is taken at sign-up.

Month-to-Month

  • Ages 0 - 2: $105/month
  • Ages 2+: $85/month
  • Family Plan: $350/month*

Annual Commitment

  • Ages 0 - 2: $150/month
  • Ages 2+: $75/month
  • Family Plan: $300/month*
While our clinic undergoes remodeling, we exclusively offer in-home visits, and all travel fees are waived for those who sign up for our membership within our service area. Enjoy quality pediatric care and the flexibility to cancel anytime.


The re-registration fee is $100 per person.

*Family Plan consist of two adult caretakers and their minor dependents. Independent memberships are charged to any additional adult caretakers (i.e. grandparents), adult children >21, or adult children who are married.

Non-Member Fees

For non-members, enjoy on-demand primary care at $300 per patient per doctor's visit without a subscription commitment. We charge a $30 travel fee for home visits within our service area. We are currently waiving the travel fee if you sign up for our membership while our clinic is under renovations. Discounts are available to uninsured, healthshares, high-deductible plan holders, and financially needy.
